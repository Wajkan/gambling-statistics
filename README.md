# Gambling Statistics
*"Take full control of your gambling"*

A personal statistics tracker giving you a complete and honest overview of your entire gambling history — across every casino, betting site and game type. See exactly where you stand, what you have won, what you have lost.

*"No one should own your statistics but you."*

This is a tool built purely to give you control — over your data and your history.

---

## What It Tracks

- Win / loss per casino and betting site
- Win / loss per slot, game or bet type
- Sports betting — full history per sport, league and bet
- Poker and any other form of betting
- Total lifetime win / loss
- Overview per platform

---

## Why It Exists

Seeing your complete history laid out clearly is one of the most powerful tools for understanding and taking control of your own behavior. Not to judge — but to inform.
